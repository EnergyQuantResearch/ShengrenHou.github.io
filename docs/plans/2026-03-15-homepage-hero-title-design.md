# Homepage Hero Title Design

**Context:** The homepage hero on the public founder site is already structurally correct, but the Chinese H1 still reads slightly too technical and blocky for a higher-brand landing page.

**Decision:** Replace the current Chinese hero title with a shorter, sharper founder-style statement:

`用 AI 与市场机制，重构能源系统的决策效率。`

**Why this option:**
- It keeps the site anchored in energy and power-market substance.
- It introduces a stronger point of view than the current system-description wording.
- It sounds more like a founder thesis and less like a product requirement.

**Scope:** Only update the Chinese homepage H1 and make a minimal supporting spacing adjustment if needed to preserve the hero rhythm.
